Digit Ratios

Welcome to the Digit Ratios App for DIY Diagnostics!
The process can be completed in a few simple steps:
  1. Upload an image of your hand (see example above).
  2. Look at the index finger. First, tap the tip of the index finger, then the point where the index finger meets the palm of the hand.
  3. Repeat for the ring finger.
  4. If you want to start over, press the "Reset" button at the bottom of the page.
  5. Press the "Analyze" button when you are satisfied with how your points are placed.
  6. Get your results!
